# Security Policy

## Reporting
Please email security@starlist.app with evidence and steps to reproduce.

## Response
We aim to acknowledge issues within 48 hours and resolve them within 14 days.

## Disclosure
We follow responsible disclosure. Please do not post vulnerabilities publicly until patched.
